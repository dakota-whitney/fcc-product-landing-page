About

MetroPark is a mobile and web application for identifying and locating street, garage, or metered parking in any metropolitan locale.

How It Works

Users update their parking status at a specific street address, which then tells the app whether that spot is free or taken. By updating their status, users would be able to earn loyalty incentives that MetroPark offers. These include:

  • Auto-insurance discounts
  • Discounts on various partner parking garages
  • Parking meter vouchers
  • Gas cards

Features

It offers features such as live updates on which side of the street is not scheduled for street cleaning that day, how much metered parking is based on your location, locations of closest parking garages, etc.
